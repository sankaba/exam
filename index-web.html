websites & webdesign

2021 "Ad Astra" website

role - logo designer & content creator


problem - In this project our main problem was the unified inexperience in website design & development. Our chosen company concept was to sell therapeutic space travelling.


solution - we stuck to a minimalistic and a general “space travelling” website design. although it did not communicate well our company’s specialty of therapeutic space travelling.

2021 "DAMHSA" website & website design

role - website designer


problem - in this project we encountered a problem that step dance is a too competitive dance. so our primary challenge was to solve this problem using the right design choices that would be as well attractive to our middle-aged target group.


solution - we came up with sleek and colorful design that communicated our company’s goal to be less competitive. Shades of green and orange translated both the Irish heritage of the dance and our schools light-hearted attitude towards the dance.

2021 "Orchil" website & website design

role - website and brand designer


problem- in this project we encountered the problem that our previous bold design choice did not fit the company concept of selling eco-conscious office supplies.


solution - we created a new, mellower brand identity, which was inspired by orchids. it proved to be a better design choice through target group testing. people were more inclined to buy our products as they felt that by doing so, they were doing good by earth.
