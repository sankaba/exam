2018 “Seaways” music video clip

role - director & producer


problem - in this project I was presented with a challenge to create a music video which would unravel the meaning of the song and origin of the band.


solution - with the tool of animation i made a music video clip which supported the overall brand of the band and their message in the song “Cliche”. artistic animations of nature and earth formed a visual meaning of such lyrics like “i was trying to find you, and i think i was close”

2021 “How to frame a picture” video clip

role - art director


problem - in this project our challenge was how to tell a compelling story hrough the prism of a “How to..” video.


solution - we took a simple idea for a tutorial and spiced with a pun. as well as that, we chose to mix in a medium of stop-motion to tell a story of how to frame a picture. in the outcome we got a charismatic and humoristic tutorial.
