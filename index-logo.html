logos & illustrations

2019 logos for “Year of school societies”

problem - i had to create a logo that would communicate core values of the Lithuaniana school society, such as enthusiasm, solidarity and knowledge.


solution - the logo variations therefore are in leafy green and ground brown colors. The logos feature human, tree and circle symbols which in unison with the colors help to allude to the mentioned values.

2019 logo for HoneyCoasta

problem - while talking to the artist Honeycoasta about his vision for an album, he said that he would like a typography based logo and that sound waves would be incorporated.


solution - the logo created is based on my handwriting and is made to look like the letters were distorted by sound waves.

2021 “Ad Astra” company logos

problem - company “Ad Astra” needed a logo that would communicate that they were a therapeutic space traveling.


solution - the logo created symbolizes the services that the company provides, which are very individualistic and mental wellness orriented. the logo includes symbols of star and a circle and unify to reveal the company’s core message "Give yourself some space".
